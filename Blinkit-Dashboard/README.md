# Blinkit Sales Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and payment trends. This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through visualizations, KPIs, and filters, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The primary objective of this project is to:

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing products and brands.
* Understand payment method preferences.
* Compare sales performance across different areas.
* Demonstrate data visualization and business intelligence skills using Power BI.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The following technologies were used to build this dashboard:

* **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI calculations and measures.
* **Microsoft Excel / CSV Files** – Data source and preprocessing.
* **Data Modeling** – Relationship creation between multiple tables.
* **Data Visualization** – Interactive charts, cards, slicers, and reports.

---

## 📂 Data Source

Dataset Source: Kaggle

The dataset consists of multiple CSV files containing sales, customer, product, and transaction-related information. These files were imported into Power BI, cleaned, transformed, and connected through data modeling to create a comprehensive sales dashboard.

---

## ✨ Features & Highlights

### Business Problem

Retail businesses need quick insights into sales performance, customer activity, and product demand to make informed decisions.

### Dashboard Features

* Interactive slicers for Category, Month, and Customer Segment.
* KPI cards displaying:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Orders
  * Total Quantity Sold
  * Average Order Value
* Brand-wise sales analysis.
* Product-wise sales comparison.
* Payment method distribution analysis.
* Customer sales performance tracking.
* Area-wise sales analysis.
* Dynamic filtering and cross-interaction between visuals.

### Key Insights

* Identifies top-performing brands and products.
* Highlights customer purchasing patterns.
* Shows preferred payment methods among customers.
* Enables comparison of sales performance across locations.
* Provides a quick overview of overall business performance.
